The 5 Rescue Steps

  1. Authenticate your domain. Set up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC so providers can verify you.
  2. Warm up your mailbox. Ramp volume gradually and build a positive sender reputation.
  3. Clean your list & patterns. Remove bounces, throttle sends, and avoid sudden spikes.
  4. Keep content simple. Plain text, minimal links/images, personalized and human.
  5. Monitor placement. Test regularly, adjust sending domains, and keep tightening.
